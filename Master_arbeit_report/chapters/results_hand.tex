\section{Hand-guided trajectory}

The euclidean norm and ICP fitness threshold used to select loop closure candidate is shown in \cref{tab:loop_closure_threshold_hand}. Forthcoming results are for 2 trajectories for each cap (2/cap, for 3 caps) at $\beta = 25mm$. High eye-in-hand calibration errors, which means estimated static transformation between marker attached to kinect is not so accurate therefore only relative 3D position error are shown in \cref{tab:3d_position_error_hand}. Trajectory comparison and ICP performance is shown in \cref{tab:trajectory_comparison_hand} and \cref{tab:icp_gt_hand} respectively. The \cref{fig:ca_124_7_hand_trajectory_summary_beta_10} and \cref{fig:cs_301_7_hand_trajectory_summary_beta_10} shows summary of ground truth and ICP estimated trajectories for cap with 63 and 23 electrodes respectively. For both images, top-left section shows front and side view of ground truth trajectory (green) recorded by attaching a marker. The sections 1-2, 3-4, and 5-6 are visible while sections 2-3, 4-5 are not. Top-right section shows front and side view of ICP estimated trajectory (red) which starts from 1 and ends at 2. The bottom-left section shows front and side view of both ground truth and ICP estimated trajectory on top of each other for easy visualization. Bottom-right section on the other hand shows a closer look at starting and ending portion of each trajectories.
